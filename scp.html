Scenario Planning - 3-in-1 Body Wash

Scenario Potential Impact Response Strategy
Increased Demand for Natural Ingredients Higher production costs but greater market appeal Source eco-friendly ingredients and emphasize sustainability in marketing
Regulatory Changes in the Cosmetics Industry Possible reformulation requirements and compliance costs Stay ahead of industry regulations and adapt formulations proactively
Rise of Competitors with Similar Products Market share reduction due to increased competition Differentiate with unique branding, customer loyalty programs, and new product variations
Economic Recession Decrease in consumer spending on premium products Introduce budget-friendly versions and flexible pricing strategies
Growth of E-commerce and Direct-to-Consumer Sales Shift from retail stores to online shopping Strengthen online presence, improve e-commerce logistics, and invest in digital advertising
