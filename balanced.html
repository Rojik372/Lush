Balanced Scorecard for Lush 3-in-1 Body Wash

Financial Perspective

Increase revenue through premium pricing and sustainable product demand. Maintain cost efficiency by optimizing supply chain operations.

Customer Perspective

Enhance customer loyalty by providing eco-friendly, multi-functional body wash. Focus on social media engagement and influencer marketing.

Internal Business Processes

Improve product innovation by integrating natural ingredients. Streamline production for sustainability and efficiency.

Learning & Growth

Invest in employee training for sustainability initiatives. Encourage R&D for new eco-friendly formulations.
