Customer Persona for Lush 3-in-1 Body Wash

Demographics

Young professionals, students, and gym-goers (ages 18-35) who prioritize convenience and sustainability. Most reside in urban areas and have a busy lifestyle.

Goals & Needs

Customers seek a high-quality, all-in-one solution that simplifies their shower routine while using natural, eco-friendly ingredients.

Challenges

They struggle with carrying multiple products, maintaining a minimalist lifestyle, and ensuring that their skincare choices align with their ethical values.

Buying Behavior

They prefer premium, ethical brands and often purchase products online or from eco-conscious stores. Subscription services appeal to their need for convenience.

Preferred Channels

They engage with brands through Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube, valuing influencer recommendations and customer reviews.
