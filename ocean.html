Red Ocean vs. Blue Ocean Strategy

Understanding how Lush’s 3-in-1 body wash is positioned in the market.

Red Ocean Strategy

Red Ocean represents the existing, highly competitive market where companies fight over the same customers. Traditional body wash brands compete on price, branding, and minor formulation differences.

Blue Ocean Strategy

Blue Ocean focuses on creating new demand by offering something unique. Lush’s 3-in-1 body wash reinvents convenience, combining shampoo, conditioner, and body wash into a single, high-quality formula for a fresh, hassle-free experience.
