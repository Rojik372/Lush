PESTEL Analysis

Political Factors

Regulations on personal care products, FDA approvals, import/export restrictions on ingredients, and sustainability policies.

Economic Factors

Consumer spending on skincare, inflation affecting raw materials, competition, and changing purchasing power.

Social Factors

Growing preference for organic and eco-friendly products, awareness of skincare, and changing lifestyle trends.

Technological Factors

Advancements in skincare formulations, sustainable packaging innovations, e-commerce growth, and AI-driven personalization.

Environmental Factors

Demand for biodegradable packaging, water conservation in production, and sustainability in raw materials.

Legal Factors

Compliance with safety standards, bans on animal testing, trademark laws, and labor laws affecting production.
