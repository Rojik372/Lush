SWOT Analysis

Strengths

Eco-friendly ingredients, versatile 3-in-1 functionality, dermatologically tested, and premium fragrance options.

Weaknesses

Limited brand awareness, higher pricing compared to mass-market competitors, and dependency on niche marketing.

Opportunities

Growing demand for sustainable personal care, expansion into global markets, and partnerships with wellness brands.

Threats

High competition from established personal care brands, shifting consumer preferences, and regulatory challenges.
