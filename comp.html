Competitor Analysis

Identifying Competitors

Our key competitors in the Indian personal care market include Nivea, Fiama, Pears, and Himalaya, each offering unique strengths in the industry.

What They Do Well

Nivea: Strong brand trust with a focus on moisturizing and skincare benefits.

Fiama: Luxurious fragrances and skin-conditioning ingredients.

Pears: Gentle, glycerin-based formula known for skin-friendliness.

Himalaya: Herbal and natural ingredients for a mild, Ayurvedic approach.

Areas for Improvement

While competitors excel in different areas, challenges such as high pricing, artificial ingredients, or lack of versatility create opportunities for innovation.

Affordable yet premium-quality product with skin-friendly ingredients.

Eco-friendly packaging with sustainability in mind.
